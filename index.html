My research covers the areas of Bayesian inference, Markov chain Monte Carlo methods (MCMC) and image processing. Currently, I am a PhD student in Heriot University under the supervision of Dr. Yoann Altmann, Prof. Jean-Yves Tourneret and Prof. Steve McLaughlin. My PhD thesis focuses in Bayesian methods for inverse problems, with particular interest in low-photon imaging applications.
